About Me

Hi, I'm Noah.

I grew up in Pittsburgh. Pittsburgh was built on the principles of strong work ethic and philanthropy, values I wholeheartedly stand behind. When the time came to select a school, I chose to attend the University of Waterloo for it's Management Engineering and Co-Op Programs.

Co-Op was extremely valuable and taught me countless lessons. I've found that I really enjoy working in the fast pace, ever-changing tech world and have gravitated towards PM roles.

When I'm not in the classrooom, you can likely find me hitting a little white ball around a grassy patch, but occasionally that ball manages to find water and sometimes even sand. I'm also an avid reader of everyhting in the business, leadership and self development genres.

The Management Engineering degree is a modern take on traditional industrial engineering. At its core, management engineering is about making systems and process more effective and more efficient. It pulls from three core areas of specialization: operations research, information systems, and management of technology.
